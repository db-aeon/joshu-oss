(pixelated hand cursor + "joshu" lockup), logo, and related brand assets (including
the hand mark and product naming conventions such as jChat, jMail, jWeb). The wordmark
is protected as both a **trademark** (brand identification) and **copyright**
(creative work).

This policy applies regardless of whether you use the AGPL community edition or
a commercial license.

---

## The name "joshu" vs. the Joshu wordmark

- **The word "joshu"** (lowercase, descriptive) may be used freely to refer to the
  Joshu product, your **personal joshu box**, or **your own joshu character** —
  the assistant persona you configure on your box. User-created characters and
  portraits are **encouraged**; they are part of the product story, not official
  brand assets.
- **The Joshu wordmark and hand mark** (official logo lockup, JDL assets) remain
  restricted. Do not reproduce them in competing products, managed-service marketing,
  or derivative branding without permission. See **Joshu Design License (JDL) assets**
  below.

---

## Permitted (no permission required)

- Descriptive references: *"Based on Joshu"*, *"Powered by the Joshu open-source box stack"*
- Personal naming: *"here's my joshu"*, *"my joshu box"*, *"meet my joshu"*
- Creating and sharing **your own joshu character** (portrait, persona, backstory) in
  docs, social posts, or community art — as long as you do not imply official
  affiliation or use the official wordmark/logo without license
- Accurate attribution in documentation, about screens, and README files
- Using the **Vanilla Box Stack** theme shipped in the public AGPL repository

---

## Requires permission

- Using **Joshu**, **joshu**, or confusingly similar marks in a **product or company name** that implies official affiliation
- **Official Joshu Cloud**, **Joshu Hosting**, or similar without a license
- Using the **Joshu logo / wordmark / hand mark** in marketing for a competing managed service
- Registering domain names or social handles that imply you are Project Aeon Inc.

Contact **info@joshu.me** for OEM, MSP, or co-marketing agreements.

## Joshu Design License (JDL) assets

Visual identity assets (paper-shell theme, icon set, design-system tokens beyond
Vanilla) are **not** AGPL. They are licensed separately under JDL and are
included only in official fleet builds or under commercial agreement.

The public repository ships a **Vanilla** unbranded theme. Do not copy
proprietary brand assets from official images into competing products.

## Enforcement

Project Aeon Inc. may enforce trademark and copyright rights independently of
software license terms. Good-faith community use that follows this policy is
welcome.
